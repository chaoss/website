### Web Content

This working group maintains the website and helps coordinate the distribution of CHAOSS messaging on internet platforms. Areas of interest include the website, Twitter, CHAOSScast, CHAOSStube, CHAOSSweekly, CHAOSSblog, CHAOSS metrics release, and press releases.

The Web Content community call meets the First Monday of each month at 11:00am CDT / 4:00pm UTC / 5:00pm CET via [Zoom](https://zoom.us/j/4998687533) -- [Agenda and Meeting Minutes](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1p079Q75RZ2Duk-nX4osXY2v3oFjqF6-BTZG6XPx8iQ4/edit?usp=sharing)
