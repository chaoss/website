# Other Nearby Open Source Events

CHAOSScon is loosely co-located with [FOSDEM](https://fosdem.org/2026/) but we encourage you to check out these other events happening in and around the area.

- [FOSDEM Fringe](https://fosdem.org/2026/fringe/) - a comprehensive list of FOSDEM related events happening immediately before and after FOSDEM
- [EU Open Source Week](https://opensourceweek.eu/) - these events are happening in Brussels between Jan 26, 2026 and Feb 1, 2026
- [EU Open Source Policy Summit 2026](https://summit.openforumeurope.org/) - happening in Brussels Jan 30, 2026

CHAOSScon is also a part of the [Open Source Village](https://opensourcevillage.org/) a two week celebration of open source hosted by our friends at the Commons Hub. There are many events happening during this time, and you can purchase an all-inclusive ticket along with your CHAOSScon ticket. You can read more about this on the [CHAOSScon EU page](https://chaoss.community/chaosscon-2026-eu/).
